Mechanic Man

Mechanic Man is a superhero that got into a car wreck. He misdiagnosed a vehicle that led up to the events of his wreck. After waking from a coma and getting back to every day life, he found out he had an uncanny ability to tell what is wrong with a vehicle just by looking at it. A seemingly useless superpower that he uses every day to help others that are in need.
